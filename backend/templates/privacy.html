Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you register or book a flight on SkyStream, we collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, age, and payment details. We also collect usage data such as pages visited, search queries, and booking history to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process flight bookings and issue digital tickets
  • To send booking confirmations and flight updates
  • To manage your account and loyalty tier
  • To improve our platform and personalise your experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (payment processors, email services) strictly to operate our platform. All third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies

SkyStream uses session cookies to keep you logged in and remember your booking preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Booking records are kept for 7 years for financial compliance. You may request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@skystream.pk. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and bcrypt password hashing to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password for your account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the platform. Continued use of SkyStream after changes constitutes acceptance.
